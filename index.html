    Vaccination Process of COVID-19

    The COVID-19 vaccine which was launched on 16th January, 2021 was in its first phase given to healthcare and frontline workers. Senior citizens over 60 years of age and individuals above 45 years with comorbid conditions were the second group to receive COVID-19 vaccine. The third phase of the COVID-19 vaccination drive for 18-44 year-olds also started recently on May 1, 2021.

    • Registration Process with First Dose

      The registration process of COVID vaccination which are available from notified government and private health facilities known as COVID Vaccination Centres (CVCs) can be done on the Co-WIN Portal (Cowin.gov.in) or on the Aarogya Setu app. Since the vaccination centres have limited on-the-spot registration facilities, it is mandatory for people in the age group of 18 to 44 years to first register and book an appointment online. The only requirement is that of a valid ID proof while registering. Once registered, a confirmation SMS is sent on the registered mobile number. One can also download the appointment slip on their smartphone or can choose to print it. Like the registration process, the rescheduling process is also very simple. One just needs to click on the reschedule tab to choose a different time or date of convenience, in case they missed the current schedule for any reason.

    • Registration for the Second Dose

      The recommended interval for administering the second dose of COVAXIN is 4 to 6 weeks whereas for COVISHIELD is 4 to 8 weeks (6 to 8 weeks offers the highest efficacy). Fresh registration needs to be done for the second dose depending on the vaccine that was taken earlier. The COWIN portal helps in providing details of a centre where the same vaccine as the first dose (COVAXIN or COVISHIELD) can be retaken.

    • Purpose of the Vaccination Certificate

      The vaccination certificate issued by the CVC gives information regarding the type of vaccine (COVAXIN or COVISHIELD) used, and in case of the first dose, also provides the due date for the next vaccination. It is a proof that can be shown to any requesting authority which includes during travels and certain social interactions.

    How To Register for COVID-19 Vaccine?

    If you want to register through the Aarogya Setu app on your phone follow these steps:
    1. Open the Aarogya Setu app on your phone
    2. Select the CoWIN tab in the app
    3. Tap on the ‘Vaccination’ option and then tap Register Now
    4. The app will ask you to verify the phone number via OTP
    5. After verifying, people need to enter Photo ID proof and more details as mentioned above.
    6. You will receive a text message with appointment details after a successful registration.



    HOW TO BOOK A VACCINATION APPOINTMENT

    The COVID-19 vaccination drive for everyone above the age of 18 begins from May 1 and the registrations for the same will start from midnight on April 28. Those eligible for receiving the COVID-19 vaccine will have to first register on the CoWIN website of the Aarogya Setu app. Here is how to register for the COVID-19 vaccine in India:
    1. Go to the CoWIN website using any browser
    2. Click the “Register/Sign In yourself” to begin the self-registration process
    3. The website will ask you to enter your phone number for receiving an OTP
    4. Enter the OTP you receive on your phone
    5. The website will then ask for details for a Photo ID proof
    6. Select a valid photo ID proof from the list (Aadhaar Card, Driving License, Pan Card, Passport, Pension Passbook, NPR Smart Card or Voter ID)
    7. Enter the Photo ID Number, Name, Gender and year of birth
    8. Once all fields are filled, click on register.
    9. After registering, the website will let you schedule an appointment with your nearest vaccination centre .
    10. You can also add three more members to the same account.
    11. Enter the Pin Code in the search bar to select your nearest vaccination centre and select the available time slot .
    12. Click confirm and you will have scheduled the vaccination appointment. You will receive a text message on the registered mobile number with the appointment details.
